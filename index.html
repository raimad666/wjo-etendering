WJO E-Tendering Registration Requirements

Before you are able to participate in Tenders, please ensure you meet one of the following criteria:

Kuwait Petroleum Corporation

Suppliers must be registered in the Commercial Pre-Qualification (CPQ) system of the Kuwait Petroleum Corporation in the State of Kuwait.

OR

Saudi Arabian Chevron Inc.

Suppliers must be registered with Saudi Arabian Chevron Inc. to be eligible for participation.
