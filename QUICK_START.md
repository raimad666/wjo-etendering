# Quick Demo Guide

## 🎯 See Your Page in Action - RIGHT NOW!

### Step 1: Open the Demo
1. Navigate to: `c:\Users\ramie\new project\`
2. Find the file: **`demo.html`**
3. **Double-click** `demo.html` to open in your browser

   OR

   Right-click `demo.html` → Open with → Chrome/Edge/Firefox

### Step 2: Explore the Features

#### ✅ Hero Section
- See the professional blue gradient header
- "Supplier Registration" title

#### ✅ Registration Steps
- Scroll down to see the 4-step process cards
- Hover over each card to see the animation

#### ✅ Registration Form
- Try filling out the form fields
- Notice the validation (required fields marked with *)
- Click "Submit Registration" to see the demo success message

#### ✅ Inquiry Form
- Scroll to the "Have Questions?" section
- Fill out and submit to see demo alert

#### ✅ FAQ Section
- Click on any question to expand the answer
- Click again to collapse
- Try clicking multiple questions

#### ✅ Responsive Design
- Resize your browser window to see mobile/tablet views
- Everything adapts automatically!
