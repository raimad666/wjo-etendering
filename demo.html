Supplier Registration

To participate in Wafra Joint Operations as a supplier, you must be registered with either the Kuwait Petroleum Corporation (KPC) CPQ in the State of Kuwait or with Saudi Arabian Chevron Inc. (SAC).

Registration Requirements

Before doing business with Wafra Joint Operations, please ensure you meet one of the following criteria:

KPC

Kuwait Petroleum Corporation

Suppliers must be registered in the Commercial Pre-Qualification (CPQ) system of the Kuwait Petroleum Corporation in the State of Kuwait.

SAC

Saudi Arabian Chevron Inc.

Alternatively, suppliers must be registered with Saudi Arabian Chevron Inc. to be eligible for participation.

Supplier Inquiries

If you have questions about the registration requirements or need further assistance, please contact us.
